The Game Without Decency

The Game Without Decency is very similar to Cards Against Humanity. If you haven't played either, this game requires a minimum of 3 players- 18 and older- where player 1 plucks a Scenario card and the remaining players play their best response card. The response card, that's chosen by player 1 as their favorite, wins the round. Each player gets a turn to play a Scenario card and pick their favorite response card.

Without a doubt, this game can get pretty raunchy...definitely not for the timid or easily offended! However, it can bring about lots of discussion and help you possibly discover new things about your friends..."
