Adult Loaded Questions

Adult Loaded Questions may cause you to learn new things about your friends...or have new insights formed, about them...

With 4-6 players, The first player has to flick the spinner, pick the next card, read the card aloud, and the remaining players must write down their answers. Once the player to the right reads the answers aloud, the first player must pick their favorite answer and match all answers to the correct players.
