Taboo

Taboo is a fun game to play, requiring 4 or more players. You only have 60 seconds and the goal is to get as many points, as possible. Trying to get your partner(s) to guess a word without saying the 5 words, beneath it, can be truly entertaining, hilarious, and fun!
